Pay via Till Number
Triggers an STK Push on the customer's phone to pay a till (Buy Goods).
ℹ In sandbox, use till number 174379. The STK Push goes to the customer's phone, not yours.
1 50 100 500
How it works
An STK Push (pop-up) appears on the customer's phone asking them to enter their M-Pesa PIN. Once confirmed, money goes to the till.
Till vs Paybill
Till numbers use Buy Goods — the customer pays directly without entering an account number. Best for shops, kiosks, and point-of-sale.
Sandbox tip
The STK Push won't actually appear on a real phone in sandbox. Use 254708374149 as the test customer phone.

Pochi la Biashara
Sends an STK Push to pay a Pochi la Biashara number.
ℹ Pochi la Biashara is for small business owners who use their phone number as their business number.
1 50 100 500
How it works
Pochi la Biashara lets small business owners receive money via their personal phone number — no till or paybill needed.
Two numbers involved
Customer phone — receives the STK Push and pays
Pochi number — the business owner's phone that receives the money
Registration
The recipient must have Pochi la Biashara activated. Dial *334# on Safaricom to activate it.
